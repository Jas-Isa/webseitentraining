About Us

Tom and I live together with our cats in a house in Hamburg. Since our cats live with us, they are affectionate and trusting. All of our furry friends are part of our small family and are treated with the same respect, appreciation, and love.

When selecting our breeding cats, in addition to meeting the breed standard, we place great emphasis on their character. The well-being of our animals is our top priority, which is why we do NOT breed with shy, fearful, too small, or sick animals. Our cats are regularly vaccinated, tested for blood groups, FeLV, Fiv, and PKD. In addition, before their first litter, an HCM scan is also performed.

If you're interested or have any questions, you can get in touch with us via our contact form or on Instagram. Please read through our information beforehand, as it often answers many questions already! ;)

About Us

We are a family-run cattery located in Hamburg, Germany, and our cats and kittens are an essential part of our family. We have a passion for breeding British Shorthair and Longhair cats, with a special focus on tortoiseshell and solid colors in shades of Cinnamon, Fawn, Lilac, and Chocolate.

When selecting our breeding cats, we not only ensure they meet the breed standards but also place great emphasis on their personalities. The well-being of our animals is our top priority, which is why we do not breed with shy, fearful, undersized, or ill animals. Our cats receive regular vaccinations and are tested for blood groups, FeLV, FIV, and PKD. Additionally, they undergo HCM scans performed by a cardiologist registered with pawpeds.

Our kittens are raised in a loving home environment, surrounded by plenty of attention and care. This helps them develop social skills and a pleasant temperament. We take the health and well-being of our cats and kittens very seriously and work closely with our veterinarian to ensure that our kittens are in excellent health, fully vaccinated, and dewormed.

We are always happy to assist you with any questions you may have and to help you find the perfect kitten for your family. Please take a moment to read through our information beforehand, as it often answers many questions already!

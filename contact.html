Info

To enquire for a kitten please provide us information about yourselves and the home you would provide for one of our babies. Please specify what you are looking for: girl / boy and colours. Please let us know which country you live in and if you are looking for a pet kitten or a kitten for breeding. Our Kitten leave with at least 14 weeks of age, may vary depending on your location. They will be vaccined, chipped, dewormed and bring their pedigree from Felidae Cat Club e.V. into their new home.
